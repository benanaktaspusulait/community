# Phase 6 — Expansion and Intelligence

## Goal of the Phase

Phase 6 expands the platform across:

- multiple countries
- multiple community types (ethnic, interest-based, professional, local)
- stronger intelligence layers (search, matching, summarization)

## Success Definition

Phase 6 is successful if:

- the platform can be launched repeatedly with a playbook
- localization works (language, region, policy)
- intelligence features improve outcomes without hurting trust

## Scope

### 1) Localization

- multi-language UX and content strategy
- locale-aware formatting (dates, currency, locations)
- policy variations by region

### 2) Multi-Community Playbooks

- templates for different community archetypes
- migration playbooks and seeded library kits
- admin enablement and operational onboarding

### 3) Intelligence Layer (Advanced)

Capabilities that can mature here:

- semantic search across threads/resources
- high-quality matching (requests ↔ listings ↔ providers)
- automated summarization and knowledge extraction (human-reviewed where needed)
- proactive duplicate detection at scale

Constraints:

- respect provenance and citations (link back to sources)
- keep “human trust” as the primary design constraint

### 4) Platform Operations at Scale

- multi-tenant controls and governance
- abuse prevention at scale
- cross-community analytics and health dashboards

## Expected Outcome at the End of Phase 6

- a global-ready community platform
- intelligence features that materially improve speed-to-outcome
- scalable operations across multiple regions and community models
